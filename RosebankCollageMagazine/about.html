About Rosebank College Magazine

The Rosebank College Magazine is a student-led publication dedicated to capturing the spirit of our campus — from achievements and creative works to interviews and career insights.

This platform gives students the voice to share their stories, ideas, and accomplishments while connecting the Rosebank community through creativity and knowledge.

About the Designer
